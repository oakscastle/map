A Church Door

When Martin Luther nailed The Ninety-Five Theses on the door of All Saints' Church, it wasn't because it was a church. During his time, the church door was the place for people in the community to introduce information into the community. This project is to build a vitrual church door, and eventually develop mechanisms for the community to mediate content.
